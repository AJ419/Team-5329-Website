Who we are

We are SA3S; the robotics team for the School for Advanced Studies and the Academy for Advanced Academics from Miami, Florida whose mission is to strive for excellence in automating the future. We aspire to lead our generation in the creation and implementation of self- sustained automatic applications that enhance human performance and forge a new path in the technological world.


Our Aspiration

Due to the rigorous college curriculum provided by both dual-enrollment programs, high school science classes are unavailable to the students at both institutions. Our robotics club, however, provides students with a hands-on learning environment to further explore the sciences. Despite not having a venue to study science, we have overcome this boundary to pursue our dreams in the STEM field. We are currently reaching out to our community to gain sponsorships that will allow us to continue striving for our goals by enrolling in competitions to demonstrate our abilities. We will use these funds for upgrading our equipment, paying registration fees, and traveling to competition sites.
